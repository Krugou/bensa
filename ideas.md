# 💡 Future Ideas & Roadmap for Bensa

A collection of brainstormed features and improvements to make the application even better.

## 🚀 Navigation & Travel

- **Route Planner:** Enter a start and end point (e.g., Helsinki to Tampere) and find the cheapest stations along your specific route.
- **Consumption Calculator:** Input your car's fuel consumption (L/100km) and tank size to see exactly how much you'd save by driving to a cheaper station.
- **Range Visualization:** Show a circle on the map indicating how far you can drive with your current fuel level (user-input).

## 📊 Data & Insights

- **Predictive Pricing:** Use historical data and global oil price trends to suggest whether you should "Fill up now" or "Wait until tomorrow."
- **Weekday Stats:** Show which day of the week is historically cheapest (e.g., "Tuesdays are 3% cheaper in Espoo").
- **Heatmap Improvements:** Toggle between "Price Heatmap" and "Station Density" views.
- **Price Gap Alert:** Notify users when the gap between the cheapest and most expensive station in their city exceeds a certain threshold (e.g., > 0.15€).

## 👥 Social & Community

- **Crowdsourced Updates:** Allow users to snap a photo of a price board or manually report a price (with a "Verified by community" badge).
- **Favorites:** "Star" your favorite stations to see them at the top of the list regardless of price.
- **Station Amenities:** Filter by stations that have a car wash, vacuum, cafe, or 24/7 shop.

## 📱 Mobile & PWA

- **Widget Support:** A small iOS/Android widget showing the current cheapest price in your immediate vicinity.
- **Push Notifications (Geofenced):** Get a ping when you drive near a station that is significantly cheaper than your usual spots.
- **Apple/Google Wallet Integration:** Add a "Fuel Alert Card" that updates its "Pass" content with current local prices.

## 🛠️ Technical Improvements

- **Offline Mode:** Improve Workbox caching to allow full map and list browsing even when the tunnel/network is flaky.
- **OBD-II Integration:** (Advanced) Connect to a Bluetooth OBD-II scanner to automatically read fuel level and suggest stops when low.
- **Dark/Light Mode Toggle:** Though the app is "Cinema Dark" by default, a high-contrast light mode for bright sunlight usage.

## 🤡 "Just for Fun" (Wacky)

- **Time Machine:** View what the prices were exactly 1 year ago today.
- **"Rich List":** A leaderboard of the most expensive stations in Finland (The "Avoid at all costs" list).
- **Fuel Type Emojis:** Custom animated icons for different fuel brands (e.g., a dancing ABC clover).
